What we offer

Three tracks, one goal

Whichever stage you're at, there's a track built around what you actually need right now.

Track 01 NAP New Arrivals Program

Settling in, with language support that meets you where you are

If you're new to Australian schooling, this track is built around the everyday challenges of language, reading and writing — not just the curriculum.

Working through language barriers in everyday classwork
Reading and writing confidence, one task at a time
A friendly face who's also navigated being new here

Offered by Mehdi and Yaqoob

Track 02 Stage 1 SACE subject support

Build the habits that carry you into Stage 2

Stage 1 is where a lot of SACE skills get built for the first time — understanding what a task is actually asking, structuring a response, and managing your own timeline.

Digital Technology Essential Mathematics English Legal Studies Business Innovation Biology Chemistry Mathematics

Offered by Mehdi, Ali and Yaqoob

Track 03 Stage 2 SACE subject support

Exam-ready, with a plan for what's next

Stage 2 support is about more than the content — it's exam technique, assessment strategy, and starting to think seriously about your pathway after school.

Subject support in Digital Technology, Biology and Chemistry
Exam preparation and assessment technique
Pathway advice for university, TAFE or work

Offered by Mehdi and Ali
