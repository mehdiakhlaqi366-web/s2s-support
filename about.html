About the program

Why this exists

Some of the most useful explanations don't come from a textbook — they come from someone who sat in the same classroom a year or two ago and figured it out.

Built on shared experience

Student to Student Support is run entirely by senior students who remember what it felt like to be stuck — on an essay that wouldn't come together, a grammar rule that never quite clicked, or simply not knowing which subjects to pick next. We offer our time to help juniors with the parts of school that don't always make sense the first time round.

Whether you're new to Australian schooling through the NAP, working through Stage 1, or deep in Stage 2 assessments, there's someone here who's recently been exactly where you are.

NAP

Support for New Arrivals Program students — language barriers, reading and writing confidence, and settling into Australian schooling.

Stage 1

SACE Stage 1 subject support — understanding tasks, structuring responses, and building toward Stage 2.

Stage 2

SACE Stage 2 subject support — exam and assessment prep, and pathway advice for what comes after school.
